ABOUT US

Home >About Us

Global Career Consultancy is situated at the heart of the capital of Dhaka, Bangladesh. We are one of the best study abroad agency in Bangladesh, placing students into Universities/Colleges all around the world. We provide complete counseling for students who wish to pursue their higher education overseas. Our team of consultants possesses comprehensive understanding of the academic institutions, eligibility factor and current developments.

Personal and financial matters relevant to both parents and students are given complete importance and taken into account before carving the due path. We act as a bridge between the university and the student, wherein we provide career counseling, visa assistance, admissions as well as pre-departure briefing to students to ensure that the students find the entire process hassle free.
